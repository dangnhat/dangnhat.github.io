Optional Vacation Projects

 These are the list of hand-on project topics that you can play with. They are totally optional and will not affect your grade at all but they are fun to play with! The project topics are just guidance so feel free to unleash your creativity or even go with your own project ideas!

It is kind of overwhelming at first so you might want to start with something small such as blinking an LED, outputting a hello world string to an LCD display, getting an input value from a button, etc. and build your way up from there.
